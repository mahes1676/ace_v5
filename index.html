About ACE

More than a club.
A launchpad.

ACE — the Association of Computing & Electronics — is the technical heartbeat of our engineering department. Since 2023, we've been turning curious students into builders, breakers, and innovators.

🎯

Who We Are

ACE is a student-driven technical club operating across three specialised wings — Artificial Intelligence, Cybersecurity, and Embedded Systems. We bring together students from all branches who share one thing: the drive to build something real.

🔬

What We Do

We run hands-on workshops, competitive hackathons, research projects, and industry-connected events. Members work on real hardware, real datasets, and real vulnerabilities — not just theory from a textbook.

🚀

Why Join

ACE members graduate with a portfolio, not just a degree. You'll build projects, earn recognition, mentor juniors, and connect with alumni working at top tech companies and research institutions.

Purpose

Vision & Mission

🔭
Vision

To be the most impactful student technical community in our institution

We envision a future where every engineering student has access to cutting-edge technical knowledge, collaborative tools, and real-world projects — regardless of their branch or background. ACE will be the bridge between classroom theory and industry-ready skills.

🎯
Mission

To nurture technically strong, ethically grounded, and project-ready engineers

Through structured learning paths, mentorship, and hands-on projects, we empower students to build solutions that matter. We cultivate curiosity, collaboration, and confidence across AI, Cybersecurity, and Embedded Systems.
